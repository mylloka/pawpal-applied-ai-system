# Reflection — PawPal+

## What I learned

I learned that testing is just as important as writing the code. When I wrote the tests, I found bugs I didn't even know were there. It made me realize you can't just assume your code works — you have to check it.

## What I would do differently

Instead of deleting tasks that have conflicts, I would try to move them to a later time slot instead. That would make the app more useful.

## How I grew

I got better at breaking a big problem into small pieces. I also learned that AI tools are helpful but not always right — I had to think for myself and not just copy what they suggested.
